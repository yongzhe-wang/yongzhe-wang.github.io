Research

My research focuses on making machines understand and act in the physical world — bridging language, vision, and action. I work across vision-language-action models, world models, and foundation-model pre-training.

Sep 2025 – Present

Vision-Language-Action Research, Penn GMLR Lab

Research on VLA and video action models (VAM) for dexterous manipulation. Exploring flow matching, diffusion policy, and action chunking for contact-rich robotic tasks. Advised by Prof. Jiatao Gu.

Apr 2026 – Present

World Model Pre-training, Noematrix

Leading foundational world-model pre-training at a humanoid robotics startup.

Jan 2025 – Jun 2025

VLA / Foundation Model Intern, 1X Technologies

Trained action-conditioned video generation on robot egocentric footage using MAGVIT2 tokenization. Built the Data Engine for teleop data processing. Helped deploy foundation models on NVIDIA Jetson Thor for NEO humanoid.

Mar 2024 – Dec 2025

AI Research Fellow, Meta

Contributed to the LLaMA family of foundation models.

May 2024 – Aug 2025

Optimal Transport & Classification, UNC-CH

Developed SliceOT, a method combining R-CDT and Optimal Transport for linearizing non-linear image distributions. Achieved 99% accuracy on sign language classification and ~94% on brain-tumor classification under small-sample regimes. Advisors: Shiying Li, Caroline Moosmüller. arXiv
